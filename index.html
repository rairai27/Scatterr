About Our Barangay

Welcome to Barangay Concepcion Pinagbakuran! Concepcion Pinagbakuran is a barangay in the municipality of Sariaya, in the province of Quezon. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 3,050. This represented 1.88% of the total population of Sariaya.

About Us

The population of Concepcion Pinagbakuran grew from 1,950 in 1990 to 3,050 in 2020, an increase of 1,100 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 1.65%, or an increase of 228 people, from the previous population of 2,822 in 2015.
